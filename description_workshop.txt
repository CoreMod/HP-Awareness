This is a simple client-side mod that contains:
[list]
    [*]Hurt and low HP/Health overlays + optional audio indicator
    [*]Player HP bar
    [*]Audio and visual indicator above you whenever potion sickness wears off
    [*]Debuff indicators above you whenever you obtain a debuff
[/list]
Every feature can be disabled or altered to your liking in the config.

Suggest/report features and bugs here or see the source code at https://github.com/CoreMod/HP-Awareness.
