This is a simple client-side mod that provides more feedback whenever you get hit or are on low health. You no longer have to stare at a health bar in the corner of your eye.

This mod also provides both an audio and visual indicator whenever potion sickness wears off.

Features can be disabled or changed in the config.


Planned features:
-Debuff icons appearing above the player whenever one is gained
